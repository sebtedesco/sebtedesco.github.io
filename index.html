Welcome! My name is Sebastian Tedesco and I'm a Technical Implementation Engineer based in Louisville, CO. I quickly discovered my passion for technology as a student at the University of Colorado at Boulder where I obtained a Certificate in Design Technologies through the College of Engineering and Applied Science.

Upon graduation, I enrolled in an accelerated Web Development program where I worked extensively with HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JavaScript (ES5/ES6+), React, and Node.js. I also utilized version control systems like Git/GitHub and collaborated on projects using Figma, MeisterTask, and Trello. Additionally, I worked with relational database technologies, including PostgreSQL and SQL.

I completed a UI Engineering internship at MK Decision and joined the team full-time as a Quality Assurance Engineer. I wrote automated scripts to perform end-to-end user interface testing, and worked closely with the Product and Engineering teams to provide feedback and collaborate on product road maps.

When MK Decision was acquired by Alkami Technology in 2021, I joined the Loan Origination department as a Technical Implementation Engineer. I was the technical point of contact for clients during their implementation process, providing technical support, guidance, and QA while implementing their environments. I built technical integrations between existing clients online banking solution, and our new loan origination platform. In addition, I built custom reports using SQL queries to PostgreSQL databases. I worked closely with DevOps, Dev, Product Managers, Project Managers, and Customer Service teams to streamline implementation processes and help improve performance and customer satisfaction.

I am particularly passionate about leveraging both my technical expertise and interpersonal skills in my daily work. I thrive in environments where I can deeply engage with the technologies I work with and serve as a technical point of contact for both clients and internal teams. I find fulfillment in building technical integrations, testing, writing documentation, and working closely with clients to offer support, guidance, and solutions to meet their needs.
